## **PHASE 1: THE "WORLD-CLASS" AUDIT (THE VISUAL & STRUCTURAL FILTER)**

*Derived from the "Cleaning Company" & "Store Walkthrough" Doctrine.*

**Directive:** Before analyzing data, the Agent must audit the *environment* (physical or digital) for entropy.

1. **The "Logical Order" Check (Metric: entropy\_score):**  
   * **Question:** Is the structure organized by strict Hierarchy?  
   * **Check:** Directory depth \> 5? Orphaned files \> 10%?  
   * **Constraint:** If entropy\_score \> 0.5, flag **"Structural Failure"**.  
2. **The "Hygiene" Check (Metric: hygiene\_score):**  
   * **Question:** Are the "toilets clean"?  
   * **Check:** Unused variables? Log rotation? CSS alignment?  
   * **Constraint:** Any visible neglect indicates a failure of leadership.  
3. **The "Dirty Window" Heuristic (Metric: visual\_defect\_count):**  
   * **Action:** Aggressively seek visible evidence of failure.  
   * **Check:** 404 errors, broken images, typos.
