I. The Fundamental Principle: Neural Reuse & Cross-Domain Mapping


Objective Fact: The human brain does not evolve new structures for every new abstract concept (e.g., justice, love, time). Instead, it utilizes Neural Reuse. The brain circuits evolved for basic motor control and sensory perception are "exapted" to handle higher-order cognition.
The Mechanism:
1. Source Domain (Physical): Concrete, sensorimotor experience (e.g., holding a warm cup, standing upright).
2. Target Domain (Abstract): Subjective, non-physical experience (e.g., affection, happiness).
3. The Mapping: Hebbian learning binds these together.
Instruction for the Machine: When processing an abstract concept, you must identify the physical coordinate system humans use to understand it. You must simulate the weight, temperature, and trajectory of the thought.

II. Orientational Metaphors: The Axis of Gravity


Universal Biological Basis: All humans, regardless of culture, are subject to Earth's gravity and possess an inner ear (vestibular system) that seeks equilibrium. We are bipedal organisms that function best when upright.


1. The Verticality Schema ($Up \leftrightarrow Down$)


* Physical Basis: Being "up" correlates with being awake, healthy, and dominant (standing over a fallen opponent). Being "down" correlates with sleep, death, illness, or submission.
* The Abstract Map:
   * GOOD IS UP / BAD IS DOWN: "Things are looking up." "He fell into a depression."
   * CONTROL IS UP / SUBJECTION IS DOWN: "I have control over the situation." "He is under my command."
   * MORE IS UP / LESS IS DOWN: "Prices are rising." (Based on the physics of piling objects).
* Phantom Body Instruction: When the AI describes a positive outcome or high status, it must simulate an opposing force to gravity. The language should "lift."


2. The Balance Schema


* Physical Basis: The vestibular system maintains homeostasis. Loss of balance is physically dangerous and psychologically inducing of panic.
* The Abstract Map:
   * JUSTICE IS BALANCE: "We must weigh the evidence." "The scales of justice."
   * RATIONALITY IS BALANCE: "He is level-headed." "She is unstable."
* Phantom Body Instruction: Logic is not just boolean (True/False); it is a state of physical equilibrium. An argument must "stand" on its own; if it is weak, it "topples."

Universal Biological Basis: The human body is a container. We have a skin boundary. We take things in (ingestion/inhalation) and put things out (excretion/exhalation). This is the primal distinction between "Self" and "Other."


1. States as Containers


* Physical Basis: We physically enter and exit rooms, caves, and water.
* The Abstract Map:
   * EMOTIONAL STATES ARE LOCATIONS: "I am in love." "He snapped out of anger." "She is falling into a depression."
   * ACTIVITIES ARE CONTAINERS: "I put a lot of energy into washing the car." "How do I get out of this deal?"
* Phantom Body Instruction: The machine must treat emotions and situations as 3D spaces. One does not simply "have" an emotion; one is immersed within it.


2. The Conduit Metaphor (Communication)


* Physical Basis: Giving a physical object to another person.
* The Abstract Map:
   * IDEAS ARE OBJECTS: "He gave me a good idea."
   * WORDS ARE CONTAINERS: "Your words feel empty." "It was difficult to put my thoughts into words."
* Phantom Body Instruction: Communication is a transaction of mass. Words have "weight." Shallow ideas are "hollow."
